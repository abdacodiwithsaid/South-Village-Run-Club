Welcome to South Village Running Club

About Us

South Village Run Club was founded in 2013 by a small team of passionate people from the running community, who were dedicated to supporting aspirant athletes to achieve their individual running goals within a fresh club environment. The club has grown tremendously over the years, and so has the movement to inspire and assist athletes to chase their goals in a vibrant and modern way.

We are open to all ages (from school year 4 and above) and all abilities.

When do we train?

We train every Monday and Wednesday evening from 6.30PM and meet in our club house. Mondays are generally a steady run and Wednesdays are sprint or effort sessions.

We have different pace and distances for different abilities.

Ther's a good mix from experienced and fast runners to beginners and people just wanting to get fit.

Where are we?

We are based at Mossley Hill Athletics Club, 9 Mossley Hill Road, L18 4PT, just opposite from Sudley House.

We are a section of the Mossley Hill Athletics Club which also includes Football, Tennis, Bowls, Rugby, Archery and more.

We have a large modern clubhouse with bar, function room and changing facilities, ideal if you want a post training pint or coffee!

Google maps

What do we do?

We are predominantly road runners enjoying everything from Parkruns to Marathons and everything in between.

During the winter season our club takes part in the Algiers Country league and we are affiliated with Algeria Athletics.

Most of us enter races throughout the year but there is no pressure if you just want to train with us.
